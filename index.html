Our Services

At RPG Gutters and Landscape, we offer comprehensive outdoor and gutter services designed to keep your property in top condition. From routine maintenance to specialized installations, our skilled team provides dependable solutions for every landscape need.

Lawn Care and Sod

Our detailed lawn care and sod services include mowing, edging, aeration, fertilization, and professional sod installation. We assess your soil and grass type to deliver a dense and vibrant lawn that thrives year-round.

Landscape Design

Our landscape design specialists craft functional and beautiful outdoor environments, integrating plant selection, hardscaping, and lighting to reflect your personal style and maximize usability all year long.

Tree Care

Keep your trees healthy, safe, and beautiful with our professional tree care services. We offer expert pruning and crown thinning to improve structure and light penetration, deep-root fertilization to boost nutrient uptake, and integrated pest & disease management to protect against threats. We also provide risk assessments, cabling & bracing for weak limbs, soil aeration, and safe removal of dead or hazardous branches—ensuring your trees grow strong and enhance your landscape for years to come.

Spring & Fall Cleanup

Prepare your landscape for seasonal transitions. Our cleanup services remove debris, trim perennials, and ready your beds for new growth in spring or shield them from harsh winter conditions.

Junk Removal

We clear unwanted items, yard debris, and household junk quickly and responsibly. Whether it’s a single bulky item or a full property cleanout, our team handles disposal and recycling for a spotless result.

Pressure Washing

Restore surfaces to their original condition with our pressure washing. We remove dirt, mold, and stains from driveways, decks, siding, and more, revitalizing your property’s appearance instantly.

Lawn Aeration

Enhance root health and overall turf quality through aeration. By perforating compacted soil, we improve water, nutrient, and air uptake, leading to a thicker, greener lawn.

Sprinkler Service

Keep your irrigation system running at peak efficiency. Our sprinkler service includes inspections, repairs, and system design to ensure uniform water distribution and conserve water usage.
